Lost and Found

Every year we find a lot of different things people have lost during the night. If you miss something and think that we could have found it - don't hesitate to contact us! You can write us an email(info@forestparty.ch) or write us a private message on Facebook

 

Is the party only for Students from Fribourg?

No everybody can come, but you have to be older than 18. So don't forget to bring your ID!

 

Where can I get my Ticket?

You can get Tickets at our two Presale Spots at University Pérolles and University Miséricorde or directly at the spot starting at 21:00 - for further Information please click PRESALE

 

Mobility

We provide a free shuttle service and there will be a parking ground! You can find further informations at Location!

 

Can I bring my own alcohol inside?

No, but we sell all of our alcohol at student friendly prices.
