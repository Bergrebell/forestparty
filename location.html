New Location 2.0

Some of the residents complained about the noise and made sure that we couldn't get the permission to host the party at the "historic" place. So we had to search for yet another location. We hiked through almost every forest in the whole region just to find you a new place which provides the same "Forestfeeling" and we are very proud to present you our new spot!

 

How to get there

You have several different possibilities to reach the party! Choose the one that fits you best!

Free Shuttle Bus:

The shuttle service starts at 9pm at the "Place de Notre-Dame" (next to the Cathedrale and the Hotel de la Rose). Our shuttle armada consists of three 13 seated mini-busses and one large 50 seated bus. They will circulate constantly till the party ends at 5am. You don't have to pay anything for the bus.

Car/Taxi:

You can reach the location - starting at the Fribourg Cathedrale - in about 6 minutes by car. So the taxi shouldn't be that expensive (~20CHF). In addition it's possible to leave the car at our special parking ground. Our security staff will give you further directions at the place.

Directions to the parking ground

For the sportsmen:

First you have to buy some beers at the train station or wherever you want. The you'll take the bus(line 6 direction Windig) till the station "Fribourg, Kessler". From there you can follow the signs that will lead you to the spot in about 40 minutes - this time for real! ;)
